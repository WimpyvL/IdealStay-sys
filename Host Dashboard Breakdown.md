### **Detailed Sub-Menu Breakdown: "Add New Listing" Flow**

This is a multi-step guided process designed to capture all necessary information efficiently.

#### Step 1: Property Foundation & Basics
*   **Property Type:** (House, Apartment, Cabin, Villa, Unique Stay).
*   **Location:** Address input with map confirmation (verifying GPS coordinates).
*   **Guest Capacity:** Number of adults, children, and infants allowed.
*   **Bed & Bath Count:** Detailed input for bedrooms, beds per room, and bathrooms.

#### Step 2: Description & Narrative
*   **Listing Title:** (Suggestion tool for effective titles).
*   **Property Description:** Rich text area detailing the experience and features.
*   **Highlights:** Checkbox list of key selling points (e.g., Pool, Hot Tub, Beach Access).

#### Step 3: Amenities & Services
*   **Standard Amenities:** Wi-Fi, AC, Heating, Kitchen, Parking, Essentials (towels, soap).
*   **Premium Amenities (Idea Stay Focus):** Dedicated Workspace, Fitness Equipment, EV Charger, Smart Home features.
*   **Safety Features:** Smoke alarm, CO detector, First Aid Kit, Fire extinguisher.

#### Step 4: Photos & Verification
*   **Photo Upload:** Bulk upload tool with required minimum resolution.
*   **Photo Tagging:** Assigning room/area labels to each photo (e.g., "Master Bedroom," "Kitchen View 1").
*   **Verification Request:** Prompt to schedule/upload videos for Idea Stay property verification (ensuring property quality matches claims).

#### Step 5: Pricing & Availability
*   **Base Nightly Rate:** The standard price.
*   **Minimum/Maximum Nightly Rate:** Boundaries for the dynamic pricing tool.
*   **Cleaning Fee:** Separate, non-negotiable fee.
*   **Security Deposit:** Policy selection and amount definition.
*   **Tax Settings:** Input for local or required occupancy taxes.
*   **Initial Availability Setup:** Set up initial blocked/available dates.
